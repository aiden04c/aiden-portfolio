What I do

Three ways I move work forward.

Hire me when something needs an owner — a stalled deal, a launch with no clear lead, a platform that has to ship. I take it from where it's stuck.

01

Client & account delivery

I own the client relationship from first discovery meeting to signed contract — proposals, pricing, cost and risk negotiation, RAID logs and change control. On fit-out work I won 5 deals and kept them on budget and on schedule through 20+ change requests.

02

Cross-border growth

I help brands cross borders. I turn scattered Douyin, WeChat and GA4 data into a quarterly product and content plan the founder can run against — then launch the overseas storefront end-to-end, with analytics on every step from day one.

03

Cloud platform delivery

I scope, build and ship on Cloudflare — Workers, Pages, KV, R2, DNS. I own content, IA, deployment and monitoring, with enough engineering empathy to demo, debug and de-risk a customer call myself.

Toolkit — what I use, and how

Win & run the work

I write the proposals and pricing, estimate the cost, and negotiate with vendors — then run delivery with RAID logs, change control and stakeholder reports in Microsoft Project, so a deal goes from first meeting to signed and stays on budget.

Measure & decide

I read the numbers in Google Analytics 4 and Google Ads, pull store data from Douyin and WeChat, then query it with SQL and model it in Excel — so campaign and sales data turns into the next decision a client can act on.

Build & ship

I build and launch the live site on Cloudflare Workers and Pages, wire up KV and R2 storage, set the DNS, and write the HTML and CSS — all versioned in Git, start to finish with no hand-off.
