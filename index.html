What I do

How I move work forward.

Hire me when something needs an owner — a stalled deal, a launch with no clear lead, a platform that has to ship. I take it from where it's stuck.

01

Project & account management

I take a project end to end: pin down the real problem, structure the proposal, price the work, and manage cost, risk and change as it runs. I keep clients, contractors, suppliers and senior stakeholders aligned to one plan, and trim cost without cutting scope.

02

Business development & sales

I pitch and win work. I shape a client's problem into a solution they will buy — a service, system or platform — build the business case, and close the deal, from government pitches to corporate contracts.

03

Data & growth analysis

I'm strongest with a messy dataset. I pull together campaign, store and web analytics, find what is actually driving results, and turn it into a clear product, content and growth plan — the basis for taking a brand into new markets.

04

Cloud platform delivery

I build and ship the live platform myself on Cloudflare — content, structure, deployment and monitoring. Enough hands-on engineering to demo it, fix it, and answer the hard technical questions in a customer meeting myself, without waiting on a developer.
